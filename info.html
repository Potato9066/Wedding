Information

  • Things To do At Wedding
    • Explore the Mansion Grounds - There will be full access to the mansion and its property and you are welcomed to explore during your time at the wedding
    • Walk to the Beach - The beach is around a 20 minute walk away from Felt Mansion. Feel free to bring walking shoes and take a trip down during Social Hour or the Reception time.
    • Play Lawn Games - There will be several lawn games set out to play or tables to sit and watch.
    • Visit the Disc Golf Course - There is also a full disc golf course on the property to play during down time.
  • Food and Drink
    • There will be various appetizers and snacks available during Social Hour including Chips and Dip
    • Dinner will be a buffet style that consists of tacos (chicken, pork, and barbacoa) from Donkey Taqueria - a favorite of many of the wedding staff!
    • This will be a dry wedding and so no alcohol will be served or permitted, however other drinks including water and pop will be available.
  • Phones during Ceremony
    • We would greatly appreciate that phones be put away during the ceremony so as to be in the moment with us and not distract other guest. However you are welcomed and encouraged to take as many pictures as you would like during the reception!
    • We will have a photographer there to capture the moments during the ceremony, so please reach out to us if you want pictures of the ceremony.

FAQs

  • What time will the ceremnony and reception be?
    • Please see the Schedule and Locations on the Venue page of this website for this information.
    • If you arrive after the ceremony has already begun, we also ask that you head directly to the mansion - our coordinator or a Felt Mansion staff will direct you on where to go when you arrive
    • If you would prefer to join us for only the reception (dinner and festivities), then simply mark your RSVP as Reception Only or text us this preference - our coordinator or a Felt Mansion staff will direct you on where to go when you arrive.
  • Where can I stay for the wedding?
    • We will not have a hotel block for this wedding, however we recomend looking at The Double Tree by Hilton or The Courtyard Marriott for lodging options.
  • What Happens if it Rains?
    • In case of light rain there will be umbrellas provided and the ceremony will continue as planned.
    • In case of inclement weather (lightning and thunderstorms), we would be forced to move the ceremony inside the Chapel which can only seat 80 people. If this occurs we ask that you be courteous to others needs, especially if you feel you are able to stand for the 20 - 30 minute ceremony.
    • Please watch the weather as our wedding date approaches to plan accordingly. We will attempt to notify everyone if the schedule is changed due to the weather. This decision will be made on 06/28/2025 by noon
